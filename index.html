// What you get

SPF record
Tells receiving servers which IPs are allowed to send mail from your domain.
DKIM record
Cryptographically signs your emails so servers can verify they weren't tampered with.
DMARC record
Instructs receiving servers on what to do with emails that fail SPF or DKIM checks.
MX records
Routes incoming mail to the right server - your email platform, Google Workspace, etc.
CNAME records
Custom tracking links, subdomain forwarding, and ESP-specific verification.
Verification report
Full deliverability check after setup - you receive a report confirming everything passes.
